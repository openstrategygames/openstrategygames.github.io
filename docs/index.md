<div class="osg-about" markdown>
Open Strategy Games are faction-scale, argument-driven games where players control organisations rather than individuals, resolve conflict through reasoned argument and probability, and build shared history through play.

*The Open Strategy Game format was defined by Chris McDowall, originating in the Matrix Game tradition established by Chris Engle and Tom Mouat. Logo by [Delapouite](https://delapouite.com) under [CC BY 3.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/).*
