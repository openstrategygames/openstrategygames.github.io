Open Strategy Games are faction-scale, argument-driven games where players control organisations rather than individuals, resolve conflict through reasoned argument and probability, and build shared history through play. This site is the central reference for the format, its rules, and its tools.

The Open Strategy Game format was defined by Chris McDowall, originating in the Matrix Game tradition established by Chris Engle and Tom Mouat.
